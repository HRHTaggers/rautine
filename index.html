welcome to rAutine

transitions can be difficult for children to understand and can make them feel anxious, concerned and distressed
this app aims to provide simple visual tools to help reduce these feelings and help your child deal with these changes by making them clear, understandable and foreseeable
no need for carrying physical boards/flashcards/timers
just one app that you can carry anywhere at any time

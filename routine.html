How to create a daily routine board
1
drag and drop the activities of your choice into the space below each timeslot
2
multiple activities can be placed in the same timeslot
3
once the scheduled activities have been completed for the timeslot, press the question mark underneath to uncover a gold star!

an application to provide a visual routine for children who struggle with transitions in their daily routine.
